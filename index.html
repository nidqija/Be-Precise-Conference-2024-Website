PRECISION MEDICINE: ONE UNIQUE STRAND AT A TIME

Why Be Precise?

Precision medicine is an emerging strategy for disease prevention and cure that considers an indicidual's genetics, biological and environmental factors as well as lifestyles. Main benefits of precision medicine includes improving disease detection, customizing disease-prevention strategies and personalising treatment.
Be Precise 2024 serves as an educational platform to introduce precision medicine to esteemed healthcare professionals, lectures, students and anyone in the medical field. The conference aims to explore the direction of precision medicine practices as well as the current practices in Malaysia. Participants will have ample opportunities to engage through our riveting Pre-Conference Workshops , Educational Talks, Interactive Activites and Lucky Draw, all designed to ignite the flames of inspiration.
